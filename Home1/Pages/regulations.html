Attendance Policy

Students must attend at least 75% of the total hours for each course (lectures and labs).

Failure to meet this requirement may result in the student being barred from entering the final examination for that course.

Academic Honors

The "Honor Degree" is awarded to graduates who achieve a cumulative GPA of 3.4 or higher.

Requirement: The student must not have failed in any course during their study at the university.
