Academic Regulations

Guidelines and policies ensuring the highest standards of academic integrity and excellence within the Faculty of Computers and Information Technology.

Credit Hour System

The faculty operates on a Credit Hour System. Each course is assigned a weight in credits, typically ranging from 2 to 4 credits. To graduate, students must complete a minimum of 144 credits, including mandatory core courses, faculty requirements, and elective courses.

GPA System 4.0 Scale
Max Load 18-20 Credits / Term

Attendance & Participation

Regular attendance is vital for academic success. Students are expected to attend at least 75% of all lectures and practical lab sessions.

  • Students falling below 75% attendance may be barred from final examinations.
  • Medical excuses must be submitted within 3 days of the absence.

Grading & Evaluation

Student performance is evaluated through continuous assessment, midterm exams, and a final examination.

Grade Percentage Points Description
A 90% - 100% 4.0 Excellent
B+ 85% - 89% 3.5 Very Good
B 80% - 84% 3.0 Good
C 70% - 79% 2.0 Fair
F Below 60% 0.0 Fail
