Latest news

Innovation University has partnered with Huawei!

Innovation University has partnered with Huawei! Through this collaboration, there will be free courses, post-graduation employment opportunities for Innovation students, and support for any student with a project idea, even if it’s small, which will help them develop it. Watch the video to know more about this partnership.

ICPC Community at Innovation University

We’re excited to have officially launched the ICPC Community at Innovation University through a session featuring top-ranking ICPC speakers from Africa and the Middle East. Dr. Lamiaa El Refaei's talk gave our students the opportunity to learn more about the competition, register for the community, and connect with some of the best ICPC programmers.

Turkish Embassy visited Innovation University

The Educational Advisor of the Turkish Embassy visited Innovation University to discuss new opportunities for students. During the visit, both sides agreed on ways to cooperate and support the students. At the end, commemorative shields were exchanged to highlight the partnership between the two sides.
